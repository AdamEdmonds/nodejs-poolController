Pentair Pool : Send Packet

To send a message, input in the box below.

Action:

For Chlorinator packets:
Enter xx,xx,xx (eg 16,2,80,0). The 98 (checksum) and 16,3 (end of packet) will be added automatically to make 16,2,80,0,98,16,3


For all Intellichlor packets:
Enter packet Dest,Src,etc. The preamble (255,0,255,165,xx,) and checksum will be added for the full packet.
Example: 16,34,134,2,9,0 (turn off circuit 9) will automatically be sent as 255,0,255,165,16,16,34,134,2,9,0,1,120
